🏡 FamilyOS - Quick Sign-In Help

📱 HAVING TROUBLE SIGNING IN?

✅ SOLUTION (iPhone/Android):

1. DON'T open link in WhatsApp/Instagram
   → Tap ⋮ menu → "Open in Safari" or "Open in Chrome"

2. Click "Continue with Google"
   → Choose your Google account

3. WAIT 2-3 seconds after redirect
   → You should see the join screen

4. Enter your invite code (or it's pre-filled)
   → Tap "Join Family Space"

---

🔧 STILL STUCK?

Try these:
• Clear browser cache
• Use incognito/private mode
• Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
• Restart your phone
• Ask the person who invited you for help

---

💡 WHY THIS HAPPENS:

WhatsApp/Instagram browsers block Google sign-in for security.
You MUST open in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android).

---

📋 QUICK CHECKLIST:

✓ Open in Safari/Chrome (not WhatsApp)
✓ Wait 2-3 seconds after Google sign-in
✓ Check internet connection
✓ Clear cache if needed
